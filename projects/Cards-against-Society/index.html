D    O    W    N    L    O    A    D


From the makers of [Software you've never heard of] comes Cards against Society, a wonderful party game for not-so-wonderful people.
Ever wanted to play a game of Cards against Humanity, but been to busy to organize a time to meet up with friends? Me neither, but it would probably be annoying. Thats why I developed Cards against Society, a free, open-source clone of Cards against Humanity, that you can play anywhere, anytime.
The app comes with over 100 white and black cards, conveniantly split into [Clean] and [NSFW]. However, if that's not enough, feel free to make your own, using the built-in deck editor, allowing for limitless customisation, and near-infinite replayability.

Get it here today.
